Hey, I am Justin!

I'm a PhD Candidate in Linguistics at CU Boulder, where I am advised by Rebecca Scarborough. My research area is phonetics, with a focus on listener design. Before coming to Boulder, I received my BA in Linguistics and Cognitive Science from Brown University. Outside of research, I like to ride trains.

I am graduating soon and seeking postdoc opportunities beginning in Fall 2025!
